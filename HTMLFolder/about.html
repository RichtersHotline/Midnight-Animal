About Us

Our History

After World War 2 a squad of soldiers decided to open up a nightclub. That Nightclub was named Midnight Animal (or Midnight Screenings in the US). For over 70 years Midnight Animal has never closed its doors. We will remain open until the last party animal vanishes. When the party animal within us all goes away only then will we shut our doors for good. But for now? We're here for you 24/7 365, always ready to give you a good time. Whether its liquor, music or just a party. We're always here for you when you need us.

Our Mission

To give you the party of a lifetime whether its weddings, bachelor parties, birthdays etc. No party is too small or big for us. Bring as many people as you want.

Each event is handcrafted by our team of planners, sommeliers, decorators, DJs and others. You can be rest assured that you can just kick back and relax and let us do all the planning knowing that your party is in good hands.

Every facet of our clubs are S-tier quality. From the floor, to the carpet to even the glasses each aspect of our clubs are of the highest quality even the smallest details.

Our Locations

Bloodline Club

The first and oldest of our clubs. Still standing after being constructed in 1947 however don't think that its outdated this club boasts the latest state of the art equipment.

Location: Paris, France

Future Club

The Future Club is our experimental club for next-gen equipment and drinks. Wanna experience something out of this world? Wanna be the first to taste a new concoction? This club is for you.

Location: Miami, USA

Simma Hem Club

Party at the top of the world at our penthouse "Simma Hem Club" (Translates to Swim Home). Why is it called Simma Hem? Well there's a pool (with no railings) at the very top of the building. Are you brave enough to go for a swim?

Location: Cape Town, South Africa

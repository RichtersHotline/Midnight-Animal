A Short Story About Us

Our story is a sort of tragic one. After the horrific events of WW2 our founder, plagued by PTSD decided he wanted to unwind. But every club he went to there were too much people who didn't appreciate what he did for his country. Too much noise did more harm than good, so he decided to open up his own club specifically for veterans. However as the years went on and the wars grew scarcer more and more non vets started coming in suddenly the club grew. Needing more hands on deck he decided to hire more people to help, DJS started flocking in and soon enough the lines reached blocks long. and thus Midnight Animal was born.

Now we have 3 clubs specifically for your enjoyment and we'll always keep the party going.
